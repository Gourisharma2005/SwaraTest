How Swara Works

Step 1: Register Complaint

Submit your complaint anonymously or with your details. Choose the category, provide a description, and attach any relevant evidence.

Step 2: Review & Analyze

Our team reviews the complaint thoroughly and analyzes the situation. We'll verify the authenticity and gather additional details if needed.

Step 3: Action & Support

Once verified, appropriate actions are taken, and you’ll receive support to resolve the issue. We ensure you are informed every step of the way.

Step 4: Resolution

The complaint is resolved with the necessary action taken. Your case is closed with proper documentation, and you'll be notified of the outcome.

About Swara

Swara is a safe space created for women to raise their voice against any misconduct or injustice. Whether it's workplace harassment, public safety, or domestic concerns — we empower you to speak up with confidence and confidentiality.
